## 🧠 My Understanding of DevOps & Cloud Engineering

**DevOps** is not just tools — it is a **culture and practice** of bridging development and operations so that software
is built, tested, and deployed faster and more reliably. It is about automation, collaboration, and continuous
improvement across the entire software lifecycle.

**Cloud Engineering** means designing, building, and managing applications and infrastructure on platforms like
AWS, Azure, or GCP — making systems scalable, resilient, and cost-efficient without managing physical hardware.

Together, DevOps + Cloud = the backbone of modern software delivery at scale.
